Hi, my name is Ashley and I have some advice for you!

Confusion is your friend!

It can be confusing when you start programming, so many things to remember! But that is ok - confusion is your brain working through something. You will get through it and you will be smarter for it!

Confusion, therefore, is a sign that you are heading in the right direction. Sometimes you want to quit - and you can - but only for a little while. Let your mind relax, but never give up. You will come back refreshed and progress forward!

Suspended disbelief plays an important role.

Sometimes you won't know why things are happening (especially with JavaScript! Oof!) but that's ok. Sometimes you need to just roll with it - one day it will make sense.

It is normal to not get things - clarity will come - and make you better for it!
